Section 04 — Strategy Deep Dive

What Goes Into Trend Aware (Original)

Layer-by-layer breakdown of the strategy pipeline — from raw monthly prices to portfolio weights and return attribution.

  • 1Input layer: Monthly prices for SPX, rates, commodities, and credit.
  • 2Indicator layer: Cycle-phase extraction (Hilbert-based) and 3M SPX momentum (rate-of-change).
  • 3Model layer: Gradient-boosted regressors estimate next-period relative edge per asset class.
  • 4Allocation layer: Equal-weight core with z-score tilts, clipped to practical allocation bounds.
  • 5Output layer: Monthly cross-asset portfolio weights translated into next-month returns.

The wave view explains the Hilbert-style intuition: cyclical indicators move in phases, and the model interprets their relative phase alignment as risk-on / risk-off regime context.
