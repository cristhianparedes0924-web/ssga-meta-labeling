"At the core of PrimaryV1 is our continuous Z-Score Composite engine. I want to emphasize that this is *not* a simplistic 'majority rules' voting system. 

It works in five distinct steps. 
1. First, we listen to four distinct macro signals: Equity Trends, Commodity Trends, Credit Spreads, and Cross-Asset Risk Breadth. 
2. Second, we normalize these inputs using an expanding historical z-score, ensuring there is absolutely zero look-ahead bias. 
3. Third, those normalized signals are aggregated into a continuous composite score.
4. Fourth, we decide. By applying strict active thresholds, if the score is above 0.0001, we trigger a risk-on BUY signal. If it's below negative 0.0001, we trigger a defensive SELL signal. 
5. And finally, we allocate—shifting into Equities and Commodities when risk is on, and retreating to safe Treasuries when risk is off.

This 4-indicator stack gives us a remarkably unified and mathematically sound directional edge."
